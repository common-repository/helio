Helio Pay ⚡⚡ Sell more with crypto ⚡⚡

- Accept crypto payments the easy way
- Set up in minutes & get paid instantly with real-time payouts
- Sell in USDC, Solana, Bitcoin, Ethereum & 100s of digital currencies
- Eliminate expensive chargebacks
- Low fees of 1%
- On / off ramp crypto earnings to your bank account

Log in to the Helio Pay dashboard with your email or crypto wallet such as MetaMask, Phantom, Coinbase or other to set up your plug-in, track orders, manage payouts and more. The Helio Pay plugin co-exists with all known checkouts for a seamless experience and can be set up easily without the need for development.

## Features

### Helio dashboard

Log in to the Helio Pay dashboard with your email or crypto wallet such as MetaMask, Phantom, Coinbase or other to set up your plug-in, track orders, manage payouts and more. See our [WooCommerce set-up documentation](https://docs.hel.io/developers/woocommerce-plugin).

### Auto currency conversion

Buyers can pay with the token of their choice.

### NFTs

Reward customers with an NFT drop. Token gate access to your store based on an NFT collection and enable discounts with an NFT.

### Community membership

Include access to your exclusive community with every sale.

### Blockchain support

Helio Pay is compatible with Solana, Bitcoin, Ethereum, Polygon, and hundreds of other tokens.

### 100% DeFi

Decentralized finance (DeFi) means instant payments to Phantom, MetaMask, Ledger, or another crypto wallet. The payment flow is peer-to-peer. No intermediary ever holds or processes your money.

### Helio Wallet

Are you new to crypto? No problem. We create a Helio Wallet for you the first time you log in.

### On and off ramps

Off-ramp digital currencies earnings from the Helio dashboard back to your bank account or card via transfers. Buyers can use credit cards payments to onramp to digital currencies and purchase in your store in a single-seamless flow

### No disputes with crypto payments

Crypto payments have a 0% dispute rate. Buyers cannot reverse payments. Chargebacks are not possible.

== Installation ==
1. Go to plugins -> add new
2. Search for Helio Pay
3. Install and activate.
3. Configure payment gateway in WooCommerce -> Settings -> Payments -> Helio

== Configuration ==
1. Log in to [hel.io](https://www.hel.io/)
2. Create a payment -> Woo Commerce. Learn more [here](https://docs.hel.io/developers/woocommerce-plugin)
3. Paste the Payment ID into the Plugin production ID field
4. Enter the API Keys saved from the Helio Dashboard into the APIk key fields
5. Save to start accepting crypto payments
